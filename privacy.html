Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Flashcard Study App ("the App") is a free educational app. This page explains our privacy practices.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All flashcard data (sets, progress, statistics) is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage.

AI Features

If you choose to use the AI card generation feature, you provide your own API key (Gemini or Groq). Your text is sent directly from your device to those third-party services. We do not store or process this data ourselves. Please review the privacy policies of those services.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Any changes will be posted on this page.
